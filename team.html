Our Expert Team

Meet the dedicated practitioners behind our holistic healing approach

Leadership

Our center is led by experienced practitioners who bring decades of expertise in Ayurvedic medicine and naturopathy.

Dr. Arjun Sharma

Founder & Chief Ayurvedic Physician

With over 25 years of experience in Ayurvedic medicine, Dr. Sharma has dedicated his life to preserving and sharing the ancient healing traditions of India. After completing his formal education in Ayurveda from the prestigious Gujarat Ayurved University, he spent several years studying under renowned practitioners in Kerala.

Dr. Priya Patel

Co-Founder & Naturopathy Specialist

Dr. Patel brings a unique blend of traditional naturopathic knowledge and modern wellness practices to our center. With a background in both Ayurveda and Western naturopathy, she specializes in creating integrative healing protocols that address the root causes of health issues.
