Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and Ayurvedic treatments.

What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient Indian system of medicine that focuses on achieving balance and harmony in the body, mind, and spirit through natural therapies and lifestyle practices.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

Our practitioners will conduct a thorough assessment during your initial consultation to determine your unique constitution and health needs, and then recommend the most suitable treatment plan.

Are Ayurvedic treatments safe?

Yes, our treatments are safe when administered by qualified practitioners. We use natural herbs and therapies that are gentle on the body and have minimal side effects.

How long does it take to see results?

The time it takes to see results varies depending on the individual and the specific treatment. Some clients experience immediate relief, while others may require several sessions to notice significant improvements.
