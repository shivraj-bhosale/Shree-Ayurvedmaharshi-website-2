Directions

By Air

The nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun, approximately 35 km away. We can arrange airport transfers upon request.

By Train

Rishikesh Railway Station is 10 km from our center. Regular trains connect from Delhi, Haridwar, and other major cities.

By Bus

Regular bus services operate from Delhi, Haridwar, and Dehradun to Rishikesh. The bus stand is 8 km from our center.

By Car

If you're driving, we have ample parking space available. The drive from Delhi takes approximately 6-7 hours.

Nearby Attractions

  • Rajaji National Park (15 km)
  • Ganges River (5 km)
  • Neelkanth Mahadev Temple (20 km)
  • Triveni Ghat (7 km)
  • Kunjapuri Temple Trek (25 km)
  • Laxman Jhula (6 km)

Accommodation

We offer on-site accommodation for naturopathy camp participants. For other visitors, there are several hotels and guesthouses within 5 km of our center.
